FAQs

How can i Become an OYO Wizard Members?

Simply purchase the membership via our web/app or at our member hotels. And please hurry, since the membership is being offered at an early bird price of 80% for a limited period of time.

Where do i see my membership details?

Your membership details are mentioned under the profile section on the web and app. Once you sign up as a Wizard member, the membership details are also sent to you through email and SMS.

I selected the option to buy a membeship but cannot see the details on my profile page

Your Wizard membership gets confirmed only post receipt of payment for the same. In case you made a Pay at Hotel booking, the membership will be confirmed only after payment is received at the hotel.

What is Wizard 'Base' hotel?

Wizard ‘Base’ is that one special hotel where you can avail 10% discount on all your future bookings.

How do I select my Wizard Base?

You will be prompted to select your Wizard Base post buying the membership & at every booking you make at a Wizard Member hotel post the purchase. All you need to do is simply select the property of your choice as Base. Please note that in case you buy membership from a Wizard Member hotel, then that respective hotel automatically becomes your Wizard Base

Can I change my wizard Base?

Once selected, the Wizard Base cannot be changed for the duration of the membership. However, if in an unlikely event of Wizard Member Hotel opting out of this program, you shall be provided an option to opt for an alternate Wizard Base.

Can I avail 10% discount for every booking at my wizard Base?

You are eligible for a 10% additional discount at your Wizard Base for every subsequent booking after your first checkout from your Wizard Base. For your first stay at Wizard Base, you are entitled for a 5% discount.

How many times can i use my membership card to avail free stay at OYO Wizard Member Hotels ?

You can avail free stay only once in every 365 days but your membership should be active at the time of redemption of Free Stay.

For free stays, wil there be any service charges payable to OYO?

For Gold, there will not be any service charges to avail the Free Stay. Whereas for Blue and Silver, the Free Stay would require a payment of a service charge, which will be communicated by OYO while the Member books a Free Stay room.

How many times can i use my membership card to avail discounts to OYO Wizard member Hotels?

Validity of OYO Wizard membership is 6 months for Blue members, 12 months for Silver members, and 24 months for Gold members from the date of purchase. You may avail discount across all Wizard Member Hotels unlimited number of times during the validity period, i.e. True Infinity!

I just became a new OYO Wizard member, Can I avail the discount benefits on my current booking?

Yes, you are entitled for a 5% additional discount for your first booking (including your Wizard Base hotel). In case you have already checked in, the same amount will be adjusted against the booking at the time of checkout

I got some OYO money after becoming a wizard member, How long will this OYO money be valid ?

OYO Wizard Blue, Silver, and Gold members will get 500, 1200, and 3000 OYO money respectively on membership purchase. This OYO money will be valid for 6 months, 6 month, and 1 year respectively for Blue, Silver, and Gold members respectively

I got Rs.500 OYO money after becoming a wizard member, When can i start using this OYO money for my booking?

This additional OYO money can be used for your future bookings across any OYO property based on the condition of OYO money application limits at the respective property. However, this cannot be applied to your current booking.
